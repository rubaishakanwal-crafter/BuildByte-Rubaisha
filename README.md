
"SkillBridge"

It is a student career guidance platform designed to help learners track skills, explore career paths, and access personalized learning resources.

The Problem:
. Students don’t fail because they lack talent — they fail because they lack a map.
. Paper Barrier – Certificates and resumes don’t prove much.
. Money Barrier – Career guidance is expensive.
. Recognition Barrier – Self‑taught skills and side projects rarely get acknowledged.
. SkillBridge exists to break those walls.

The Solution:
. One connected platform where students can:
. Test skills through subject‑based quizzes
. Earn a Digital Passport that auto‑updates with verified skills and projects
. Build a Project Portfolio that persists across visits
. Explore a Learning Hub with curated resources and roadmaps
. Take a Career Quiz that turns results into direction, not just labels


Features:
1.Student Dashboard – Overview of progress and activities
2.Career Passport – Personalized career roadmap
3.Skill Tracker – Monitor skill development
4.Learning Portal – Curated learning materials
5.Project Recommendations – Suggested projects based on skills
6.Career Quiz – Interactive quiz for career insights
7.User Authentication – Secure login and signup system

Design & Motion:
SkillBridge feels alive: smooth scrolling, animated counters, glowing buttons, confetti on quiz completion, and a flipping Passport card. Motivation is built into the UI.

Tech Stack
. HTML5 – structure
. CSS3 – styling, animations, responsive layout
. JavaScript – interactivity, quiz logic, localStorage persistence
No frameworks, no build tools — runs directly in the browser for easy demo.
